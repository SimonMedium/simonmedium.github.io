My Journey

My journey into Spiritualism wasn't something I planned or expected. It came through my own life experiences, times of loss and moments that made me look deeper into the connection between this world and the next.

Over time, Spiritualism became more than an interest. It became a path of learning, understanding and service. Mediumship has taught me that we never stop developing, and that every connection with Spirit is an opportunity to bring evidence, comfort and a reminder that love continues.

Today I have the honour of serving Spiritualist churches and venues across the UK, sharing evidence from Spirit with compassion, honesty and respect. I believe mediumship should be approached with humility, integrity and a genuine desire to help others.

Alongside my church work, I host The Circle@Greenside, a welcoming space for those exploring Spiritualism, those beginning their own journey and mediums who have been developing for many years.

Serving Spirit

I serve Spiritualist churches and venues across the UK, sharing evidence from Spirit with compassion, honesty and respect.

My aim during every service is to provide evidence of the continuity of life, bringing forward messages from Spirit while offering comfort, encouragement and a reminder that love continues beyond this life.

Every venue and congregation is unique, and I approach each demonstration with humility, preparation and a genuine desire to serve those attending.

I work with churches, open circles and special events. If you would like to discuss a booking or find out more about my work, please get in touch.

Open Circle

The Circle@Greenside is a welcoming space for anyone interested in exploring Spiritualism, whether you are just beginning your journey, looking to understand more, or have been developing your own mediumship for many years.

Our aim is to create an open and friendly environment where people can come together, share experiences and continue learning in a supportive community.
